Player Activity Log

This plugin monitors public COMM messages to log player activities within your current map view. It captures actions like captures, deployments, links, and fields, then stores them locally for review.

It's a powerful tool for tracking agent actions, monitoring regional activity, and gathering intelligence over time.

Features

  • Automatic Activity Logging

    Passively listens to the public COMM channel and parses events. It automatically ignores MACHINA activity and only records actions from Resistance and Enlightened agents.

  • Interactive Log Viewer

    A comprehensive dialog, launched from the toolbox, serves as your activity dashboard. It features a sortable list of all logged players and a detailed activity feed for the selected player.

  • Detailed Information

    Each log entry includes the player's name, faction, the type of activity (e.g., captured, deployed), the portal name, and a precise timestamp. Clicking a portal name in the log will center it on your map.

  • Log Management Controls

    The viewer includes powerful tools to manage your data:

    • Pause/Resume Logging: Temporarily stop or restart the logging process.
    • Export to CSV: Download all logged activities as a CSV file for backup or external analysis.
    • Clear Data: You can delete logs for a single player or wipe the entire database clean.

  • Local Storage

    All data is stored directly in your browser's localStorage, ensuring it persists between sessions without needing an external server.

How to Use

  1. Click the "Activity Log" link in the IITC toolbox to open the main viewer.
  2. The dialog will appear, showing a list of players on the left. The number next to each name indicates the count of their logged activities.
  3. Click on a player's name to see their detailed activity feed on the right, sorted from newest to oldest.
  4. To delete a single player's data, hover over their name in the list and click the × icon.
  5. Use the buttons at the top of the dialog to Pause/Resume, Export CSV, or Clear All data.

Important: All activity data is stored locally in your browser. It is not synced across devices or accounts. If you clear your browser's site data, this log will be permanently erased. Please use the "Export CSV" feature regularly to back up important data.
