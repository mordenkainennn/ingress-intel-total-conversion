All Portal Names

All Portal Names is an enhancement for the original "Portal Names" plugin. It allows users to force display all portal names on the map, bypassing the default collision detection that hides overlapping labels.

Features

  • Anti-Collision Bypass: When enabled, shows every portal name regardless of how crowded the area is.
  • Zoom-Level Threshold: Set a specific zoom level to trigger the "Show All" behavior, preventing map clutter at far distances.
  • Persistent Settings: Your preferences are saved in your browser's localStorage.
  • Quick Toggle: Access settings directly from the IITC sidebar (Toolbox).

Understanding Zoom Levels

Zoom levels in Ingress Intel (and IITC) represent the magnification of the map. Higher numbers mean you are zoomed in closer to the ground.

Zoom Level Intel Data Detail Visibility Context
13 - 14 Major Link/Fields City view. Far distance.
15 L4+ Portals Neighborhood view.
16 All Portals Street view. This is the default recommended threshold.
17 - 18 High Detail Block view. Extremely crowded areas.
20+ Micro Detail Individual portal precision.

Why use a threshold? At zoom levels like 13 or 14, thousands of portals may be visible. Showing all names would cover the entire map in text and potentially slow down your browser. We recommend setting the threshold to 16 or higher.

How to Use

  1. Find the "All Portal Names" link in the sidebar (Toolbox) and click it.
  2. Check "Enable showing ALL portal names".
  3. Set the Minimum Zoom Level (e.g., 16).
  4. Click Save. The map will refresh and labels will appear according to your rules.
